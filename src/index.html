Anjali Taneja

Anjali is a 22-year-old singer/songwriter based in the greater Washington, D.C. area. Born and raised in Washington, D.C., Anjali trained in Indian Classical and Jazz music from a young age. She cites her strongest musical influences as Sam Cooke, Celine Dion, and Etta James. Having relentlessly listened to Motown and 80s R&B throughout her upbringing, Anjali strives to reintroduce both 70s and 80s soul music by exploring intersections among urban sounds, old-school melodies, and even some Indian ragas. She recently released her first single, “Tell Me Why,” on all music streaming platforms. Anjali has performed at Wolf Trap National Park for the Performing Arts, George Mason Patriot Center, and various US embassies. In June of 2016, she was invited to perform with legendary jazz fusion guitarist and four-time Grammy nominee, Stanley Jordan.

Anjali - Live at Morningstar (Acoustic Session)

Live at Morningstar is a soulful 3-song, live EP with old-school jazz and blues influences and some sensibility derived from the Indian classical tradition. The EP features live acoustic versions of two of Anjali's original tracks and one track entitled "Fool That I Am", included as a tribute to one of Anjali's biggest musical inspirations, Etta James.
